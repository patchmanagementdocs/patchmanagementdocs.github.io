What is Patch Management

Patches are essenetial to the security of your organization. You are only as strong as your weakest link. It only takes one threat to take your organization down.

Why do patches need management?

Applying the patch is easy, getting all the ducks in a row, thats the hard part.

What if I just don't patch?

You are making your environment open to cybersecurity threats. Hacks are only increasing, in cost and frequency. Its not going to get safer.
